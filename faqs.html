Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How experienced is Coach Dame?:
A: Coach Dame has been playing soccer for 17 years and coaching for 3. He has worked with Impact FC and Langtree Charter Academy, and he hopes to continue coaching for a long time!
Q: How long are practices/lessons?:
All sessions will be 1 hr length, unless otherwise discussed!
How often are lessons offered?:
A: Lessons are offered as frequently as twice a week, as much as schedules align.
Q: Are prices different for individuals and groups?:
A: All pricing can be found on our programs/pricing page in the navigation menu.
Q: How long do people usually continue with lessons?:
A: People continue with lessons anywhere from 3 months to the course of years; it depends on what one looks to get out of the program.
