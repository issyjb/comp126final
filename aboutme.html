About Me

Learn About the Coach:

Hi, welcome to Dame Time Coaching! My name is Damian Hurtado, and I’m the head coach at Dame Time. I’ve been playing soccer for 17 years and coaching for 3. I’ve worked with Impact FC and Langtree Charter Academy in the past, and I currently offer small group and private training sessions. I am passionate about soccer, and I love to watch my players grow! My coaching values include integrity, honesty, hard work, and dedication. I believe that everyone has the potential to learn and grow, and my job as a coach is to guide players into becoming their best possible selves. I hope to see you on the field!
